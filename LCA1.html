 VOLCANIC EXPLOSION!!!


    Our student Ronit Miranada of class VII-B has created this amazing volcano simulation along side his teachers Mrs.Laal and Mrs.Gupta.
     CAN YOU BELIEVE that he has used simple kitchen ingredients to create this amazing wonder of science!
   


    Ingredients used:
    

        

            Ingredients
            QUANTITY
        

        

            Baking Soda
            2-3 tablespoons
        

        

            Vinegar
            1/2 cup
        

        

            Food Color
            A few drops
        

    

    
    


     STEPS TO CREATE THE EXPLOSION:
    
    
        
  1. Make a volcano shape using cardboard and paper.
    2. 
        
  2. Place a small container at the top of the volcano to hold the ingredients.
    3. 
        
  3. Add 2-3 tablespoons of baking soda into the container.
    4. 
        
  4. Add a few drops of food color for effect.
    5. 
        
  5. Pour in about half a cup of vinegar into the container.
    6. 
        
  6. Stand back and watch the eruption!
